-	Extended Sick Leave 
-	Reasonable Accommodation and Interactive Process
-	Leave of Absence with Pay
-	Misuse of Leave
-	Certificated Personnel Leaves
-	Legislative Leave

-	Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA)
